How to get to M

M is easy to reach by public or private transport. We’re about 10 minutes from the A27. And the 105 and 106 buses run to and from both Hilversum and Bussum railway stations. Below you’ll find our address details to plan your route in Google Maps.

Parking

Under the media building, you’ll find a car park with 366 spaces, including charging stations for electric cars and e-bikes.

Accessibility

In general, M’s buildings are all easily accessible. This includes the toilets, lifts and main entrances. M is also accessible in the sense of hospitality. We are always delighted to welcome guests into our home.
